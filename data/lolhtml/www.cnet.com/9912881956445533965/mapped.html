The Batman: First look at Robert Pattinson in the Batsuit

Director Matt Reeves shares a video revealing the first look at Pattinson as Batman.

Bonnie Burton
February 13, 2020 3:40 PM PST

The Batman doesn't hit theaters until 2021, but DC Comics fans got their first look at actor Robert Pattinson dressed as Batman, thanks to Director Matt Reeves.

On Thursday, Reeves shared a video via his Twitter of Pattinson wearing Batman's intimidating superhero costume, with the caption "#TheBatman #CameraTest."

The dark black and red video pans over the Batman symbol on the chest of the outfit and then we see Pattinson in Batman's cowl, looking rather ominous. The video also has a rather foreboding music soundtrack.
