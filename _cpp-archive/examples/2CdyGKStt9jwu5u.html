By Courtney Gisriel

Cupid's hard at work getting ready for the romantic holiday of the year. With hearts and roses filling the thoughts of love-struck celebrants, we could only be talking about Valentine's Day.

If the festive occasion somehow slipped your mind, fear not! You've still got 10 days left to pick up something thoughtful for that special someone.

To help you pick the perfect gift, we've scoured the internet, reached out to market research groups and retailers alike and looked at dozens of products to find out what most popular gifts are going to be this year.

We've narrowed down the list and selected the 13 hottest gifts to show your sweetheart how much you care. Read on below for the Valentine's Day edition of TODAY's Hot Holiday list.

For Her

  • 1. Orolay Women's Thickened Down Jacket, $130, Amazon

Orolay Women's Thickened Down Jacket

$130

We wrote about this coat in January and it quickly became one of the most-purchased items of the month. Moms and bloggers love it because of the many pockets, water-resistant fabric and overall stylish appearance. Show your Valentine some love by grabbing her this super popular jacket.

  • 2. Hoya Hearts, $26, The Sill

Hoya Heart in Ezra Planter

$26

When it comes to Valentine's Day, plants are the new flower arrangements. According to a recent article on Refinery29, searches for plants and plant care have grown by 97 percent and online plant marketplace The Sill is at the forefront of this green trend. These Hoya Hearts are the perfect way to treat the one who has your heart. The company also offers a subscription service with new deliveries available for $35 a month.

If you are looking to go the traditional flower route, The Bouqs Company is a great online resource for finding blooms that make great gifts.

  • 3. Initial Necklaces, $34-36, BaubleBar

Charmed Initial Necklace

$36

Delicate and personalized pieces are the hottest jewelry trend for the winter. These dainty initial necklaces from BaubleBar are a great representation of the style. With new designs released every week, the retailer has plenty of other options that fit the look like these:

  • Alpha Ice Pendant, $36, BaubleBar
  • Initial Bar Bracelet, $34, BaubleBar
  • Lexis Pendant Necklace, $36, BaubleBar

For Him

  • 4. True Grit Quarter Zip Pullover, $72 (originally $145), Nordstrom

True Grit Quarter Zip Pullover

$72

Searches for cozy gifts, like sweatshirts, sweatpants and pajama sets, have seen double digit growth according to market research firm NPD Group. These True Grit pullovers are exactly the type of cozy gift that any guy would be thrilled to receive during a chilly February.

  • 5. Hydro Flask 32-Ounce Growler, $45, Amazon

Hydro Flask 32-Ounce Growler

$65

The award-winning Hydro Flask Growler is a great option for any beer connoisseur. The double-walled vacuum insulation can keep drinks cold for up to 24 hours and the trademarked Fresh Carry system ensures that drinks stay carbonated to the last drop.

And if it's anything like Hydro Flask's travel mug, we'd bet this one will be a favorite for years to come.
